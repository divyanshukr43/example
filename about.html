ABOUT US

Respect for Animals campaigns against the cruel and unnecessary international fur trade, believing fur farming and trapping to be morally indefensible


Welcome to our sanctuary, where compassion meets action and every pawprint matters. We are a dedicated team of animal lovers on a mission to make a difference in the lives of our furry friends. Our journey began with a simple belief: that every animal deserves love, care, and a chance to thrive. Founded in 1998, our organization has been a beacon of hope for animals in need. Whether they're abandoned, abused, or neglected, we open our doors and hearts to provide them with a safe haven and a second chance at happiness. At the core of our philosophy is the understanding that animals are sentient beings deserving of respect and dignity. We strive to uphold this principle in everything we do, from rescue and rehabilitation to adoption and advocacy. Each life we touch is a testament to the power of compassion and the resilience of the animal spirit. Our sanctuary is more than just a place—it's a community of kindness, where volunteers, staff, and supporters come together to make a difference. With their unwavering dedication and generosity, we're able to provide essential care, medical treatment, and enrichment for our animal residents. But our work doesn't stop at our sanctuary gates. We are committed to creating a more compassionate world for all creatures, and that means advocating for animal welfare on local, national, and global scales. Through education, outreach programs, and legislative initiatives, we strive to effect meaningful change and raise awareness about the issues facing animals today. None of this would be possible without the support of compassionate individuals like you. Whether you donate, volunteer, or simply spread the word, you are helping to make a difference in the lives of animals in need. Together, we can build a brighter future for every paw, hoof, and wing that crosses our path. Thank you for joining us on this journey of compassion. With your support, we can continue to be a voice for the voiceless and make the world a better place for all beings.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

The killing of tens of millions of animals each year for nothing more than the fur on their backs is wrong. It’s immoral and a crime against nature. Gandhi said ‘The greatness of a nation and its moral progress can be judged by the way its animals are treated.’ Animals killed for their fur are either trapped in the wild or reared in fur factory farms in tiny, barren wire cages. All this for a product nobody needs. Respect for Animals is here to give a voice to the countless and voiceless animals whose only crime is to have been born with beautiful fur. We campaign peacefully but effectively and have a good record of achievement, securing a ban on fur factory farming throughout the UK and we have been at the forefront of the consumer campaign in the UK that has led to the closure of most fur outlets here. But there is so much more to do. The fur trade is massive, ruthless and a bully but Respect for Animals is determined to do whatever it can to bring it to an end. The killing of animals for their fur is wrong and should be banned. Respect for Animals is working to persuade consumers not to buy fur and lobbies for legislation to make the selling of fur illegal. Many countries including the UK already ban the main methods of producing fur and so they stand accused of hypocrisy by allowing the trade in and sale of fur items. We stand for animals' inherent right to live free from harm and exploitation. Our ethos revolves around compassion, recognizing animals as sentient beings deserving of respect and protection. We advocate for their welfare, opposing cruelty in all its forms, whether in factory farms, laboratories, or entertainment industries. We strive to promote ethical treatment, fostering environments where animals can flourish in their natural habitats or with responsible guardians. Through education, legislation, and advocacy, we seek to dismantle systems of oppression that exploit animals for profit or pleasure. Together, we champion a world where humanity extends its empathy to all creatures we share the planet with.
